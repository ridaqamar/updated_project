Hello, I’m Bernadette Titi Duggan

A lawyer by training and profession, called to the Bars of two Common wealth jurisdictions, Nigeria and Canada, the author, Bernadette Titi Duggan began ministering and teaching the of Word of God as the Children Church pastor in 1991 and was ordained a pastor in 1993.

Since then, together with her husband, Alexander, she has planted and nurtured churches in Africa, Europe and North America. Bernadette Titi Duggan, in her first published works, Human Genealogy and Human Flagship, introduces the reader to a whole new dimension of human history from a compelling, scientific perspective- an astonishing, breathtaking and completely fascinating vista, indeed.
